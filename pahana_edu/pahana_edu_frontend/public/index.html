Getting Started

Welcome to the Pahana Edu Bookshop Management System. This system helps you manage customers, inventory, and billing efficiently.

Customer Management

  • Add new customers with unique account numbers
  • Edit customer information as needed
  • View customer transaction history
  • Search customers by name or account number

Item Management

  • Add books and products to inventory
  • Update stock quantities and prices
  • Categorize items for better organization
  • Monitor low stock items

Billing Process

  • System automatically calculates totals
  • Generate and print bills
  • Track payment status

  • Monitor customer activity
  • Track inventory levels
